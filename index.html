Welcome to ACAEPL

Over a Decade of Industry Experience

ACAEPL is a subsidiary of Accruepole Group, a prominent EPC company in India, boasting over a decade of industry experience. We cater to diverse EPC and turnkey project needs of clients in varied sectors, including Manufacturing, Infrastructure, IT, Real Estate, Corporate, Banking, Education, and Hospitality, among others.

In 2013, Accruepole Group began as an EPC company. In 2015, we diversified to become a supplier, distributor and integrator of related products for all major domestic and global brands. Established in Mumbai by a group of engineers, Accruepole has since grown to have a presence in more than 5 states in India.

...

Our dedication led us to be recognized among India’s top 5000 MSME companies for Quality Excellence in 2019 and 2020. In 2024, Accruepole Group decided to segregate its distributorship/wholesale business and EPC business. The EPC business is a part of ACAEPL (Accruepole Construction And Engineering Pvt Ltd).

Financially and technically robust, we undertake small and large-scale projects encompassing Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Fire Protection and Plumbing works, among others. Supervised by qualified engineers and aligned with international standards, we are committed to delivering distinctive finishes and leveraging our extensive experience to ensure customer satisfaction.

Utilizing modern technologies and techniques, we prioritize timely project delivery and total customer satisfaction. Our commitment to excellence has earned us accolades from prestigious institutions for outstanding performance and quality construction.
